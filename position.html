Implementation Analyst

Boulder, Colorado

Dātu Health makes data-rich digital tools to help people and their healthcare providers achieve better health, richer lives and smarter decisions. We thrive as a diverse and collaborative team of engineers, designers, marketers and healthcare experts.

The Implementation Analyst is a critical member of the project team and will be assigned to specific client engagements. This role will collaborate with the client as well as multiple functions within Dātu to ensure solutions meet our client’s needs and also fulfill Datu’s business model and roadmap.

Responsibilities

  • Coordinate completion of project lifecycle tasks and activities at the client site. This includes but is not limited to the following Client facing requests in order to leverage a consultative approach to identify, fulfill, and oversee the completion of requests from the client. Ensure client expectations are understood and properly established based on the priorities and capacity of the team. Fulfill requests within the appropriate Dātu process so they are visible, prioritized, and addressed in a timely manner

  • Planning - assist with release planning , including product roadmaps, project plans, and task lists

  • Requirements & Design - assist the product management team with the gathering, review, and confirmation of requirements & design

  • Quality Assurance – facilitate testing of the solution in the client environment to ensure readiness for user acceptance testing

  • User Acceptance Testing - Assist the project manager in the planning and coordination of this activity to ensure the client tests the solution effectively and to ensure it meets business needs Deployment - coordinate and fulfill activities to ensure the solution is ready and properly deployed to production during go-live

  • Support - assist with the transition of the release/solution to the appropriate client support resources. Additionally, address issues related to systems that are the responsibility of Dātu to ensure the solution is functioning properly within the client environment

  • Maintain current knowledge of Dātu’s products to ensure this knowledge is disseminated appropriately at the client site

  • Participate in road mapping work sessions to ensure the interests of the client are considered in future releases

  • Assist with the gathering of status from Boulder-based functions so they are represented accurately and timely within client-facing deliverables and reports

  • Develop and maintain appropriate client relationships and liaise with the appropriate client teams to ensure the Dātu solution is - properly integrated within the client environment

  • Completes assigned tasks and addresses issues as assigned by the program/project manager or Dātu leadership

  • Travel up to 75% required

Qualifications / Skills

  • Proven experience with all phases of a healthcare systems implementation lifecycle

  • Advanced experience in business analysis and related methodology, tools, and approaches. Demonstrated ability to liaise between the client and the project team

  • Advanced experience in healthcare systems implementations. Strong problem solving skills with attention to detail

  • Strong verbal communication skills including the ability to facilitate meetings with senior client leadership and handle difficult situations

  • Strong written communication skills including the ability to clearly articulate key information within status reports and presentations

  • Strong knowledge of healthcare in order to communicate effectively with healthcare leaders and clinicians.

  • Strong interpersonal skills and demonstrated ability to develop relationships with multiple personalities

  • High level of flexibility and adaptability. Ability to work with a team in a fast paced, constantly changing entrepreneurial organization

  • Ability to prioritize effectively and meet strict deadlines while managing multiple, competing tasks

Education / Experience

  • Requires a Bachelor’s degree or equivalent related work experience

  • 8 years enterprise-level consulting and or equivalent experience

  • 5 or more years of business and technical analysis experience within the healthcare industry
